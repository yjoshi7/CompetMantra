Understanding National Payments Corporation of India and its payment systems

About NPCI

The National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) is an umbrella organization for operating retail payments and settlement systems in India. It was established with the guidance and support of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and Indian Banks' Association (IBA).

Key Facts

  • Founded by 10 banks as a initiative of RBI
  • Registered under Section 25 of the Companies Act, 1956 (now Section 8 of Companies Act 2013)
  • Headquarters in Mumbai, Maharashtra
  • Governed by the Payments and Settlement Systems Act, 2007
  • Objective: To encourage digital payments in India

NPCI Payment Systems & Tools

NPCI has developed various payment systems that form the backbone of India's digital payment infrastructure:

UPI

Unified Payments Interface - Instant real-time payment system facilitating inter-bank transactions.

Key Features:

  • Launched in 2016
  • 24x7 availability
  • Instant money transfer
  • Examples: Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm

BHIM

Bharat Interface for Money - UPI-based payment app developed by NPCI.

Key Features:

  • Government-backed UPI app
  • Simple and secure interface
  • Supports multiple languages
  • Direct bank-to-bank transfers

BBPS

Bharat Bill Payment System - Integrated bill payment system offering interoperable bill payment service.

Key Features:

  • One-stop solution for all bill payments
  • Covers electricity, water, gas, telecom, etc.
  • Standardized customer experience
  • Instant confirmation of payments

NFS

National Financial Switch - Largest network of shared ATMs in India.

Key Features:

  • ATM network connectivity
  • Maintains ATM software
  • Originally developed by NTRO (2008)
  • Now managed by NPCI

NACH

National Automated Clearing House - Web-based solution for bulk transactions.

Key Features:

  • Successor to ECS (Electronic Clearing System)
  • Faster processing than ECS
  • Used for EMIs, pensions, insurance premiums
  • Supports auto-debit facilities

AEPS

Aadhaar Enabled Payment System - Bank-led model allowing online transactions at PoS terminals.

Key Features:

  • Aadhaar and biometric authentication
  • Works with micro-ATMs
  • Balance inquiry, cash withdrawal, deposit
  • Financial inclusion initiative

APBS

Aadhaar Payment Bridge System - System for routing DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) payments.

Key Features:

  • Aadhaar-based payment system
  • Used for government subsidy transfers
  • Supports NREGS payments, pensions
  • Eliminates intermediaries in benefit transfer

NUUP

National Unified USSD Platform - Mobile banking service without internet.

Key Features:

  • Works on *99# USSD code
  • No internet required
  • First introduced by BSNL in 2010
  • First NPCI tool launched

CTS

Cheque Truncation System - Process of stopping physical movement of cheques.

Key Features:

  • Digital image-based cheque processing
  • Reduces clearance time
  • Enhances security
  • Eliminates physical movement of cheques

NETC

National Electronic Toll Collection - Electronic toll collection system.

Key Features:

  • FASTag-based toll payment
  • Seamless travel on national highways
  • ₹3000 pass for 200 rides (cars with white plates)
  • Reduces congestion at toll plazas

RuPay

Indian domestic card payment network - Similar to Visa and Mastercard.

Key Features:

  • Domestic card scheme
  • Lower processing fees
  • QR code payment support
  • Promotes financial inclusion

IMPS

Immediate Payment Service - Instant interbank electronic fund transfer.

Key Features:

  • 24x7 instant money transfer
  • Available through mobile and internet banking
  • Real-time fund transfer
  • NPCI's first immediate payment service

UPI Variants

UPI has evolved with specialized variants to cater to different user needs:

UPI Lite

Launched in 2023 for small-value transactions without PIN authentication.

Features:

  • ₹5000 maximum wallet limit
  • No PIN required for transactions
  • Works without internet
  • ₹2000 maximum per transaction
  • Initially couldn't return money to bank (now resolved)

UPI Circle

Feature for group payments and shared expenses through main account holder.

Features:

  • Simplified group payments
  • Shared expense management
  • Centralized through main account

UPI 123

UPI service for feature phones without internet connection.

Features:

  • Designed for keypad phones
  • Balance inquiry
  • Transaction capabilities
  • No internet or PIN required

Feature NACH ECS
Processing Time Faster processing Slower processing
